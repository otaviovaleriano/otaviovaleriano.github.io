Overview

Purpose

It is a website that offers information about a place called Roller Skaters World. This website will show all the different kinds of options of roller skating that the company offers such as the traditional way or the one with diversity of obstacles and challenges. Don’t you ever wanted to challenge yourself to try something new or to do something harder and different with the abilities you already have mastered? This is what the RSW company does, they provide the opportunity to do something different, nice, and safe.

Audience

Our Target Costumers are College Students that are living in Rexburg and region, and want to experience and gather unforgettable moments with their friends.

Students that want to try an inside activity to waste energy, hang with their friends or just try something different than the normal roller skating.

For example, George, that is 23 years old, he is from New Hampshire and is attending BYUI-I, currently taking classes and working part-time on campus. He wants to do something different and indoors to keep warm, with his friends that wouldn't consume too much time and money, and he wants to pay an affordable and fair price for it.

The costumers will access our website using the browser, with their computers or smartphones. This way no memory/storage use is required.

About Roller-Skating History

Joseph Merlin, a Belgian, invented the first roller skates in 1750 when he came up with the notion of making skates that could walk on the ground like ice skates. Merlin succeeded to develop roller skates with only one wheel on each foot after several attempts, and as soon as the creation was complete, the inventor decided to give a triumphal presentation to the local society to show off his achievement.

Roller skates, despite being an intriguing concept, did not gain popularity soon. Many unidentified inventors, on the other hand, continue to improve Merlin's notion. M. O Petitbled was the first to patent the new roller skates in France in 1819, and Roberto John Tyers was the second to patent the "Rollito" model in 1823. The "Rollito" was defined in the patent document as a "device with wheels attached to shoes, boots, or other element that covers the foot, for the purpose of locomotion and pleasure."

Five wheels were used to create the "Rollito," which was designed in a straight line. It immediately attracted the public's interest and didn't stop getting more complex after that. Despite their popularity, roller skates took a long time to attain widespread acceptance. It was only recognized in 1840 in Germany, for example. Customers were lured to a store by having girls serve them on skates at the time.

From 1863 onwards, with all the modifications that were gradually added, the skates grew increasingly similar to the so-called traditional skates or "quads" (two pairs of parallel wheels, fixed on two axles, on each foot) as they are generally known.

Skating Modes and Unique Challenges

Traditional Roller-Skating

Roller Skaters World gives you the opportunity to come and enjoy the traditional roller-skating experience. With a gigantic area free of obstacles, you will have the opportunity to enjoy your time with your friends or friends to be. With different styles of songs to enjoy, we provide a new experience every time you come to the Roller Skaters World. With DJs and a selected and diversified playlist you will have an amazing experience with us.

Obstacles and Challenges

In the Roller Skaters World, you will have the chance of do something different than the usual and push yourself into challenging experiences with your roller skate. We provide different ways and environments to help you to get the best experience in the roller-skating world. You can either overcome the challenges and obstacles by yourself or try with friends. The Roller Skaters World provides judges and instructors to help you and your friends to have a fair and involving experience. You have never done it before? Don’t worry, we will help you with the first steps and you’ll choose the difficult level of each challenge.

With different challenges and events happening every day, the Roller Skaters World offers different options for all kind of publics, having some restrictions or requirements for the most difficult challenges.
