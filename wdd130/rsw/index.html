Get Something Different Out of a Classic Sport!

The Roller Skaters World is decorated with lighting and decorations from the 1970’s and 1980’s, but also with modern themes that change occasionally. It all to provide you a different vibe and experience every time you go. The joyful environment is enhanced by resident and guest DJs, monitors and teachers on the dance floor teaching roller skates, an American Diner-style cafeteria, and retro arcade devices. Visitors can either bring their own skates or rent "Quad" skates, which have four parallel wheels. Contact Us – Form, Button and Social Medias icons.
